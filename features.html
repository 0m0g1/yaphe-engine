Features

From design visualizations and cinematic experiences to high-quality games across PC, console, mobile, VR, and AR, Unreal Engine gives you everything you need to start, ship, grow, and stand out from the crowd.


Some features are Beta or Experimental, and should not be considered production-ready. Check the linked documentation for details.

  • This supports soft body dynamics

    Handling deformable objects like cloth, rubber, or fluids.

  • This supports soft collision-detection

    Determining when and where objects collide, and calculating the resulting forces.

  • This supports soft constraints

    Providing mechanisms to constrain the movement of objects, such as joints, hinges, or springs.

  • This supports soft particle-systems

    Simulating the behavior of large numbers of small particles, often used for effects like fire, smoke, or explosions.

  • This supports kinematics

    Supporting kinematic animations, allowing objects to move according to predefined paths or constraints without physical simulation.
